Commissions and Contests

Competed against other canadites in Hunterdon County Vocational School District to work closely with teachers to create the front cover illustration for the annual Del Val District Arts Festival. The design was used for banners, pamphlet and brochures, posters, and invitation and placement cards. I designed the art itself using marker and pen, and then the artwork was manipulated in Photoshop and InDesign to create the various versions to be used to advertise for the festival.

Connected through the Bridging the Gap program to a client in designing a logo for a rental property. I created thumbnails, a final line drawing, and a color comp made for approval in the preliminary design phase, communicating and making variations to the clients specificactions, and then moved to Illustrator to complete the design and package it. client.

3D/Physical Art

Modeled a 'Spectator' creature using air-dry modeling clay, 4 small wooden and one plastic ping pong ball. After modeling the creature I also created a base for it to sit on to appear as if it was floating. Once the clay was dry, I painted the pieces and attached them together.
