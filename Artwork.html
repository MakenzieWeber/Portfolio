Commissions and Contests

Competed against other canadites in Hunterdon County Vocational School District to work closely with teachers to create the front cover illustration for the annual Del Val District Arts Festival. The design was used for banners, pamphlet and brochures, posters, and invitation and placement cards. I designed the art itself using marker and pen, and then the artwork was manipulated in Photoshop and InDesign to create the various versions to be used to advertise for the festival.
