Cockatoo Expression Sheet

This piece was one of the first drawings I completed in my journey of learning Krita. I had wanted extra practice using the application so I was sketching out a bunch of ideas of what I could draw. The concept began with this small doodle in the corner of my brainstorming page. I decided to develop the concept more as I liked the idea of using a bird companion that was not the more 'classical' witch comapanion of raven, crow, or owl.

Throughout the process I learned a lot, by watching more tutorials on the features in Krita and through simple trial and error until I made it work. In the end it was a fun experience to practice using a the newer application, as well as practice in drawing an person, which is a subject I have wanted to practice doing more.
